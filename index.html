Required Supplies

Students should come to class prepared with the following materials to ensure a productive rehearsal environment.

  • ✓

    1/2" or 1" Black Binder

    For music and handouts. Must not be shared with other classes. Used binders are available in the classroom!

  • ✓

    Pencils

    At least two workable pencils are required at all times. Please sharpen before class.

Performance Uniforms

A professional appearance is essential for all performances. Below are the uniform options. Please note what is provided by the school versus what you must purchase.

Option 1: Suit

PURCHASE: Black dress shirt (long sleeve, button-down, collared), black slacks, black dress shoes.

PROVIDED: Black Tuxedo Jacket. Silver Tie (Mastersingers only).

Option 2: Dress

PURCHASE: Black, close-toed dress shoes.

PROVIDED (Treble/Vocal/Titan): Southeastern Apparel Corelli Dress.

PROVIDED (Bella/Mastersingers): Stage Accents Avelle Women’s Dress.

Option 3: Informal Performances

Chorus Shirt and Khaki pants or shorts and neat tennis shoes/flats.
